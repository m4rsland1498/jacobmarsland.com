Why I Took This Subject

I took Computer Science A-level to consolidate my knowledge and understanding of computers, learn more, and become more competent in coding. I want to be able to contribute my part to a digital world because I have a strong interest and passion in computing of all sorts.

Non-Exam Assessment (NEA)

For my NEA, I developed a "Micromouse Simulator". Micromouse is a robotics competition where small, autonomous robots race through an unseen maze. My simulator captures pathfinding and physics whilst keeping an intuitive UI.

NEA Challenges Included:
• Custom Maze-solving Algorithms
• Physics Engine Integration
• Intuitive UI Design

I chose this project to challenge myself with tricky, unique elements like maze-generation and real-world logic.
