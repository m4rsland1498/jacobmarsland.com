About Me

Hi, I am Jacob. This website has been made to be a home for my CV for any applications that I make for degree apprenticeships; I am looking for an apprenticeship in either software development or cyber security.

I love computers and technology. I enjoy coding and creating; I like to consider myself a very creative person. A large part of this comes from my love of films as a canvas for art. In fact, I am currently in the process of writing and creating an animated short film made from entirely hand-drawn images. I go the cinema very often as it is simply my favourite place to be. I love it for the art, the big screen, the incredible speakers, the social viewing, and the purest form of movies.

Another form of art I love is photography. I enjoy all forms from abstract to street to graphic design. I enjoy to tell a story, portray a scene, and create emotions. I've shown some of my work below.

Back on the computer side of things, I love learning and overcoming logical problems which computers are just perfect for. They are also a place for me to utilise my enjoyment for mathematics.
